HYDRA Pinoy Division

Recruitment and Diplomacy Web Portal
Marvel Future Fight Alliance (Android & iOS)

HYDRA Pinoy Division is a small group of like minded agents based in the Philippines who play as an Alliance in the Android/iOS game Marvel Future Fight. We focus on getting our agents's heroes (or villains) up to speed with the latest information and techniques to survive both PvE in Multiplay and PvP in the Arena. We welcome both Free-to-Play users as well as VIPs.

Agents Recruitment

We require all agents of this alliance to be able to communicate in filipino, contribute at least 10,000XP to the Alliance weekly, and have at least 50,000 Alliance Battle Points weekly. The weekly requirements should be met by 10:30pm every Thursday. Joining our Alliance is really easy, as long as you do all of the following:

  • Contact us here OR
  • Apply in-game by searching for "HYDRA PinoyDiv" in the Marvel Future Fight's Alliance Menu
  • Optional: Include your Viber Number (if you have one) if you want to join our discussions.
  • Note: This is a Filipino language only alliance.
