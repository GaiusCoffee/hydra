HYDRA Pinoy Division

Recruitment and Diplomacy Web Portal
Marvel Future Fight Alliance (Android & iOS).

HYDRA Pinoy Division is a small group of like minded agents based in the Philippines who play as an Alliance in the Android/iOS game Marvel Future Fight. We focus on getting our agents's heroes (or villains) up to speed with the latest information and techniques to survive both PvE in Multiplay and PvP in the Arena. We welcome both Free-to-Play users as well as VIPs.

Agents Recruitment

We require all agents of this alliance to be able to communicate in filipino, as well as have at least one rank four (4*) level 40 hero or villain. Joining our Alliance is really easy, as long as you do all of the following:

  • Contact us with a screenshot of a Team Lineup. Your username should be clearly visible in the screenshot, as well as at least 1 4* level 40 hero/villain.
  • Apply in-game by searching for "HYDRA PinoyDiv" in the Alliance Menu.
  • Include your Viber Number (if you have one) if you want to join our discussions.
  • This is a Filipino language only alliance.

Note: All of the above are required in order to be considered for approval.
