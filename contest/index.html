How to Join (and Win)!

  • Open Netmarble Games' Marvel Future Fight App, and go to the Team Play Screen
  • Choose one of your characters that is not a Summoner. Summoners are the top characters in the current metagame, and are therfore banned from this contest. We reward creativity :)
  • Successfully finish the Team Play Stage.
  • Take a picture of your Team Play Results screen.
  • The picture should have:
    1. Your Agent Name
    2. Your Damage rating
    3. Your Team Play Elapsed Time
  • Repeat for all of your other characters.
  • Look at all of your Team Play Results pictures for today, and choose the one with the highest DPS. Every player can only submit one picture per day, using the form below.
  • After seven days (by 12/18/2015), the Four Winners will be selected by comparing the highest DPS of all of the valid submissions.
  • Good luck!

Mechanics:

  1. This Contest is open to the public, as well as to the HYDRA PinoyDiv Alliance agents (HYDRA PinoyDiv will not be accepting members until this contest has finished).
  2. All Contest submissions must be submitted within the contest dates (12/10/2015 and 12/17/2015), using the correct method (linked above) on the correct day.
  3. Summoner Characters (Loki, Sister Grimm, Coulson, Kingpin (Both Uniformed & Not Uniformed) and Ultron (Both Uniformed & Not Uniformed)) are not allowed to be used.
  4. DPS (Damage per Second) is computed by dividing the DPS Value on the Teamplay with the Seconds elapsed.
  5. Only one submission is allowed per day. If multiple submissions belong to one player on one day, then the submission with the lowest DPS value will be counted.
  6. Submissions that failed the Teamplay (no elapsed time visible) are not counted.
  7. The highest DPS that you have accumulated over the course of the event will be your entry. Only four winners will be selected by 12/18/2015- Highest and Seventh Highest DPS for the public, and Highest and Seventh Highest DPS for HYDRA PinoyDiv Agents.
  8. Mechanics may be changed without prior notice; This webpage will always be updated if this occurs. Also, HYDRA PinoyDiv Alliance Leader reserves the right to disqualify.
